PhyloRNA: a database of RNA secondary structures with associated phylogeny

PhyloRNA contains known RNA secondary structures associated with their phylogenetic information according to several curated taxonomies (SILVA, ENA, LTP , NCBI, GTDB) to allow the scientific community to study the relationship between RNA structures and phylogenetic ranks. In addition, structural features and abstractions of the structures are available.
